Wake up informed, every morning.

Morning Briefing is a personal AI alarm that replaces your standard alarm sound with a 3-minute spoken news briefing — generated fresh each night while you sleep.

Each morning you wake up to a calm, radio-style summary of the top stories from left, center, and right-leaning sources, read aloud in a voice you choose.

Once a week it skips the news entirely and plays something completely random — a deep-dive into an interesting topic, just for fun.

Works on iPhone, Android, Mac, and Windows. Takes about 10 minutes to set up.
